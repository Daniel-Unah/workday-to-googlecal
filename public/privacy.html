Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

Data Collection

This app processes your schedule data locally in your browser. When you choose to connect to Google Calendar:

  • We store OAuth tokens temporarily to access your Google Calendar
  • We use your schedule data only to create calendar events
  • Uploaded files are processed in memory and not stored

Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted sessions and expire after 24 hours of inactivity. We do not permanently store any personal information, schedule data, or calendar content.

Google API Services

This app's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy .

Your Rights

You can disconnect your Google Calendar at any time, which immediately deletes all stored tokens. You can also revoke access through your Google Account settings.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Data is only sent to Google Calendar API when you choose to add events to your calendar.
