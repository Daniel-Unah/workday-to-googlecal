Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Workday to Google Calendar Converter ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Google OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens to authenticate with Google Calendar API on your behalf
  • Session Data: A temporary session identifier to maintain your connection
  • Uploaded Files: Excel/spreadsheet files containing your Workday schedule (processed temporarily)
  • Calendar Data: Course information from your uploaded file to create calendar events

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating with Google Calendar API to access your calendar
  • Creating calendar events based on your Workday schedule
  • Deleting calendar events you've previously created through our app
  • Maintaining your session during your use of the App

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth Tokens: Stored securely in encrypted sessions (production) or local filesystem (development)
  • Uploaded Files: Processed in memory and immediately deleted after conversion
  • Session Data: Automatically expires after 24 hours of inactivity
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not permanently store any personal information, course schedules, or calendar data

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Calendar API: We send your calendar event data to Google's servers when you choose to add events to your calendar
  • No Other Third Parties: Your data is never shared with advertisers, analytics services, or other external parties

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

This App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Disconnect: You can disconnect your Google Calendar at any time using the "Disconnect" button
  • Token Deletion: Disconnecting immediately deletes all stored OAuth tokens from our system
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke our app's access through your Google Account settings at any time
  • Data Deletion: All session data is automatically deleted after 24 hours or when you disconnect

8. Cookies and Session Management

We use session cookies solely for maintaining your authenticated state while using the App. These cookies are essential for the App to function and expire when your session ends or after 24 hours.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: your-email@example.com
GitHub: github.com/Daniel-Unah/workday-to-googlecal

12. Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens only for the duration of your active session (maximum 24 hours). Uploaded files are deleted immediately after processing. No data is retained after you disconnect or your session expires.
